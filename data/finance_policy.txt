GENAI ENTERPRISE COPILOT
FINANCE AND ACCOUNTING POLICY MANUAL
Version: 3.0
Effective Date: January 2026

------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------
This document defines financial governance, budgeting, expense management, and compliance standards for the organization.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. EXPENSE MANAGEMENT
------------------------------------------------------------
Eligible expenses:
- Business travel
- Client meetings
- Software subscriptions
- Office supplies

Submission rules:
- Claims must be submitted within 30 days
- Receipts are mandatory for all claims above ₹500

Approval hierarchy:
- Manager → Finance → Department Head

------------------------------------------------------------
3. TRAVEL POLICY
------------------------------------------------------------
Domestic Travel:
- Budget limit: ₹10,000 per trip
- Economy class only

International Travel:
- Requires VP approval
- Business class allowed for flights exceeding 6 hours

Accommodation:
- Preferred hotels with corporate tie-ups
- Max limit: ₹7,000/night (domestic)

------------------------------------------------------------
4. PROCUREMENT POLICY
------------------------------------------------------------
Procurement thresholds:
- Below ₹50,000: Manager approval
- Above ₹50,000: Finance Head approval

Requirements:
- Minimum 3 vendor quotations
- Vendor evaluation based on cost, quality, and delivery timelines

------------------------------------------------------------
5. INVOICING AND PAYMENTS
------------------------------------------------------------
All invoices must include:
- GST number
- Vendor details
- Payment terms

Standard payment cycle:
- Net 30 days

Late payment penalties will be handled as per contractual agreements.

------------------------------------------------------------
6. PAYROLL MANAGEMENT
------------------------------------------------------------
Salary processing:
- Last working day of each month

Components:
- Fixed pay
- Variable pay
- Bonuses (performance-based)

Deductions:
- Income tax (TDS)
- Provident Fund (PF)
- Professional tax

------------------------------------------------------------
7. BUDGETING AND FORECASTING
------------------------------------------------------------
Each department receives an annual budget allocation.

Review cycle:
- Quarterly reviews
- Monthly variance tracking

Variance beyond 10% requires justification.

------------------------------------------------------------
8. AUDIT AND COMPLIANCE
------------------------------------------------------------
Audit types:
- Internal audit: Quarterly
- External audit: Annual

Compliance areas:
- GST regulations
- Income tax laws
- Corporate governance standards

------------------------------------------------------------
9. FRAUD PREVENTION
------------------------------------------------------------
Zero tolerance for financial fraud.

All suspicious activities must be reported to:
finance-compliance@genai-copilot.com

------------------------------------------------------------
END OF DOCUMENT
